Our Services

Trusted Scanning & Women's
Health Solutions

USG

Ultrasound scan is safe and radiation free. It uses high-frequency sound waves to create detailed, real-time images of internal organs, tissues and blood flow. We offer all kinds of USG scans & Doppler studies.

ECG

Our ECG service provides a quick and painless way to check your heart’s activity with advanced equipment and expert care.

X-ray

Our X-Ray services provide clear and accurate imaging for fast diagnosis. With advanced digital equipment, we ensure quick results.

Antenatal Care

Antenatal care is essential for protecting the health of women and their unborn children. It aims to ensure that pregnant women are offered regular checkups , information and support By Dr Arpi Sagar Lodha.

Gynaecology Treatments

Gynaecology problems related to menstrual problems , Polycystic ovarian disease, fibroid uterus, ovarian cysts and any other gyneacology related disorders.

Delivery Counseling

Dr. Arpi Sagar Lodha offers personalised guidance during antenatal visits, helping mothers choose between natural birth, C-section, or painless labour. Each option is discussed with care to ensure a safe and comfortable delivery experience.

Infertility Support

Dr. Arpi Sagar Lodha offers compassionate care, counseling, and personalised treatment plans to help couples overcome infertility and achieve their dream of parenthood. Each step is guided with empathy, medical expertise, and emotional support.

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

Dr Arpi sagar lodha delivers proper diagnosis and treatment for normal delivery , C section , painless labour , High risk pregnancy , Postnantal care , Birth control measures and abortion care services.
